About Us

We are pleased to introduce us as RAMSON ENGINEERS , a newly formed engineering company in the field of design, manufacturing, supply, erection and commissioning of industrial boilers.

Mentor of this company is a mechanical engineer having 22 years of experience in erection, project execution and purchase departments in leading boiler manufacturing companies in the field of boiler repairing, modification of boilers supply and erection of new multi-fuel smoke tube boilers and water tube boilers up to 150 TPH, 110 Kg/cm 2 and 540 o C.

Now we have entered in to a engineering business to serve better services to sugar, power, chemical, food, textile and paper industries using our good engineering knowledge and field experience. Initially, we have started to undertake following major jobs in boilers and auxiliaries.

  1. Erection and commissioning of boilers.

  2. Boiler modifications / up-gradations.

  3. Boiler repairing / re-tubing.

  4. PRDS and Steam piping

  5. APH tubes replacement of boilers

  6. Chemical cleaning of boilers.

  7. Internal and external cleaning of boiler tubes.

  8. Internal cleaning of tubes in boiling house

  9. Replacement of tubes in boiling house.

  10. Refactory and insulation of boiler/ piping /boiling house
